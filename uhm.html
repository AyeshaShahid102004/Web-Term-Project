Are you homeless or at risk of becoming homeless?

Raising the profile

We gather evidence and stories from our member organisations about homelessness causes and solutions. We share this learning with decision makers and the general public to help build a movement to end homelessness for good.

Help end homelessness

  • Homelessness includes various forms of housing insecurity, not just sleeping rough.
  • Some groups are treated as priorities based on their circumstances.
  • Common causes include relationship breakdowns, the end of a tenancy, and high housing costs.
  • Homelessness can lead to isolation, poor physical and mental health, substance abuse, and barriers to healthcare access.
  • The pandemic demonstrated that homelessness can be tackled effectively.
  • Estimates and data provide insight into the number of people experiencing homelessness in England.
  • Support is available for individuals at risk of becoming homeless.
  • There are practical ways to help individuals sleeping rough.
  • You can help end homelessness by donating, volunteering, campaigning, and more.

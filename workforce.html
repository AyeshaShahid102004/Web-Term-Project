Supporting the homelessness workforce

As the membership organisation for homelessness sector, Homeless Hope works in the interests of the workforce. There are thousands of people working in homelessness and adjacent sectors. The work can be incredibly rewarding but also comes with many challenges.We help our members to recruit and retain the very best staff and volunteers to deliver the best services. We also research and advocate on behalf of the workforce.

I believe this membership allows a voice to be heard that best represents the sector, its very best practice, and the most informed vision for the future.
